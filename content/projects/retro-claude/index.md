A skill and telemetry store layered on top of the Claude Code CLI. After each working session, a `/clear-review` slash command captures a structured retro into YAML, classified against a closed-set ontology, with mandatory evidence citations for every self-assessment. The corpus accumulates a learnings catalogue and telemetry over agent usage, friction patterns, and gotchas.

The goal is to give agentic engineering the same retro discipline a human team would have, while keeping the substrate cheap enough to use after every session, not just the big ones.
