BlobLife is a gamified habit-tracking app designed for people with ADHD. It splits your day into four phases — Morning, Work, Cool-down, Life — turns habits into a living ecosystem, and rewards consistency with cosmetics, XP, and a growing habitat garden, all centred around a blob companion that reflects how the day is going.

Under the hood: an eval-tested LLM call discerns habits, projects, and quests from morning and evening journal entries, breaks them into atomic steps, and surfaces them back to the user as suggestions for the day and week.
